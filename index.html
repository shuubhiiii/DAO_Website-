FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Gwalior DAO is a community driven, decentralized initiative focused on building a Web3, blockchain, and innovation ecosystem in the Gwalior region.

DAO stands for Decentralized Autonomous Organization. In our context, it represents a community-led initiative where decisions are made collectively by members.

Anyone interested in Web3, blockchain, and innovation can join! Whether you're a student, developer, entrepreneur, or simply curious about the space.

We organize hackathons, meetups, workshops, campus connects, conferences, and collaborative learning sessions focused on Web3 and emerging technologies.

Our vision is to establish Gwalior as a prominent hub for Web3 innovation in Central India, fostering talent, creating opportunities, and building a sustainable blockchain ecosystem.
