About The Event

India Blockchain Week Gwalior Edition

India Blockchain Week (GwaliorDAO) is India's premier regional blockchain gathering, held from December 1-7, 2025. It's a weeklong celebration featuring global Web3 gatherings by ETHIndia, Aptos, Polygon, and more.

We bring together builders, developers, creators, and innovators from across the country to explore emerging technologies, collaborate with global ecosystems, and build meaningful opportunities together.
